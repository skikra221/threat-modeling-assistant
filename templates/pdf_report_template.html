Methodology

The analysis follows the STRIDE framework for threat categorization. Each threat is evaluated using a custom scoring formula:

Risk = Impact × Probability × Exposure

Scores range from 1 to 125, mapped to levels: Low (1-25), Medium (26-60), High (61-100), and Critical (101-125). All threats are cross-referenced with OWASP MASVS standards.
